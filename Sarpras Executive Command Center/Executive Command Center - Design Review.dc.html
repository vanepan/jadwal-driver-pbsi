02 — Executive Mood System
One layout, five operational states
Mood is communicated through wording, weight, and the ring's own contrast — never a new color outside the existing semantic palette, never a flashing or dramatic treatment.

Healthy → generous quiet, no emphasis. Good → same green ring, softer claim, zero attention items — proof the layout survives an empty state gracefully. Warning → one word carries the accent color, nothing else changes. Critical → the ring's contrast steps up and the headline becomes an imperative sentence; wording carries urgency more than color does. No Data → the ring and metrics simply don't render; the page stays calm rather than showing zeros.
03 — Motion & Interaction Language
Every motion answers a question
Frozen before/after pairs — this is the specification, not a live demo. One easing family throughout: cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1). Nothing animates unless it explains what changed.
